Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 200g Plain Flour
  • Pinch of fine salt
  • Milk & Water (1 pint, do not use all)

Method

  1. Sieve flour into big bowl with the salt.
  2. Make a dip into the middle and add the egg into the dip.
  3. Slowly mix the egg and flour.
  4. When fully mixed, add milk and water while stirring.
    • Should have thick consistency.
    • Hand blender to get rid of lumps.
  5. Refrigerate for over 15 minutes.

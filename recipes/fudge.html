Fudge

Ingredients

  • 150g Butter
  • 410g Evaporated Milk
  • 500g Caster Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla Essence

Method

  1. Pour evaporated milk into a medium pot.
  2. Add the butter.
  3. Add caster sugar.
  4. Put stove on high heat and bring to a boil.
  5. Stir for 20 Minutes
    • Put off heat when thick consistency.
    • Whisk until for 10 minutes until sticky texture
  6. Add vanilla essence
  7. Whisk for another 5 minutes
    • Greaseproof paper and flatten out.
  8. Leave 2.5 hours to set and store in fridge

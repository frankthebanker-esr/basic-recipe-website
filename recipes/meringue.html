Meringue

<2>Ingredients
  • 4 Large Egg Whites
  • 115g Caster Sugar
  • 115g Icing Sugar
<2>Method
  1. Heat the oven to 110Â°C.
  2. Line 2 baking sheets with non-stick liner.
  3. Tip 4 large egg whites into a large clean mixing bowl. Beat them on medium spped with an electruc hand whisk until the mixture resembles a fluffy cloud and stands up in stiff peaks when the blades are lifted.
  4. Turn the speed up and add the caster sugar, a dessertspoonful at a time. Continue beating for 3-4 seconds between each addition. It's important to add the sugar slowly at this stage as it helps prevent the meringue form weeping later. The mixture should be thick and glossy.
  5. Sift one third of the 115g icing sugar over the mixture, then gently fold it in with a big metal spoon or rubber spatula. Continue to sift and fold in the remaining icing sugar a third at a time. The mixture should not look smooth and billowy.
  6. Scoop up a heaped dessertspoonful of the mixture. Ease it on to the baking sheet to make and oval shape.
  7. Bake for 1.5-1.75 hours until the meringues are a pale coffee colour.
  8. Leave to cool on the trays or a cooling rack.
